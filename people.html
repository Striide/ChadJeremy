Coromoto Diaz, Senior Art Director & Designer

Coromoto began her career working for the Science Museum of Caracas. Following this, she worked for over seven years in magazine publication as both art director and designer for GP and Climax—directing teams of journalists, photographers and illustrators. Coromoto’s inventive approach has been used for clients such as Eldorado Gold, Finning and Kodak. She absolutely adores her beautiful, twin girls.

Everett Ranni, Designer

Before joining Porcaro, Everett designed storyboards for several national brands including Rogers, Labatt, Sears Canada, CIBC, the NHL, the Calgary Stampede and Holt Renfrew. His work has been featured in Applied Arts Magazine as well as selected by the Association of Registered Graphic Designers to be on display at the Design At Work Western exhibit. He loves the taste of sweet & heat together in food.

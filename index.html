What We Do

As a boutique creative agency, WIAN partners with clients as closely as possible, not as many as possible. We tailor originality to your objectives. We design with purpose, setting your brand apart from the competition while always grounded in practicality and functionality. Measures of success can be an upswing in brand recognition or market gains. But our fave is a client’s slow-spreading smile. That drives us each day.

How We Work

Once the brief is in place and the goals are established, we conceptualize inside out for the best strategic solution. Your initiative needs to deliver at the right time to the right audience. Context is key. So we offer integrated services as well as à la carte. Whatever process is best for your success.

To refresh the brand and reflect the company’s pillars as service-oriented, organized, professional and friendly, the positioning line of "BC's Leader in Residential Management" was selected to clarify Baywest's offer and industry position. Minefinders

This client required a new logomark balancing both the conservative and entrepreneurial spirit of the company. We synchronized these seemingly contradictory traits with a stylized "M" to pay homage to corporate heritage, in a round embrace to signify that Minefinders has come full circle from explorer to producer, centred in future growth. The use of upper case, heavy typography conveys overall strength and sophistication, with an earthy brown and gold palette reflective of industry positioning.

New Gold

Following the merger of three companies, a cohesive identity was required for the chosen name of "New Gold.” Our approach was to incorporate a classic reference to gold's history with a ‘twist" to convey the company’s unique positioning. The timeless serif font in lower case is both distinguished and friendly, with a stylized drill bit graphic for sector positioning. The new identity seamlessly extended to annual and sustainability reports, tradeshow display, print and collateral. The tagline "a clear direction" was also developed to reinforce the company's strategy of aggressive growth through acquisition.

NovaGold

To mark a historical turning point and name change, NovaGold consigned us to develop a new identity. The design needed to reflect the company’s focus on gold as well as asset holdings of one of the top seven gold deposits in the world. The selected identity is bold and confident, showcasing the concept of discovery within the letter “A.” The design application extended to stationery, website, factsheet and tradeshow booth.

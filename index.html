Barb Moreira

Principal

Since joining Porcaro in 1997, Barb has provided communications consulting for many of BC's top organizations including BC Ferries, Creo, Finning, Goldcorp, HSBC, Placer Dome and Teck.

Barb oversees Porcaro's overall operations as well as actively works with Porcaro's clients. Her ability to stay on top of the details while maintaining big picture thinking makes Barb an asset on large initiatives.

Before joining Porcaro, Barb consulted at a web design firm and worked as an investor relations specialist for small cap companies. She is an active member of the Canadian Investor Relations Institute (BC Chapter). Barb has a BA with a Communication major from Simon Fraser University.
